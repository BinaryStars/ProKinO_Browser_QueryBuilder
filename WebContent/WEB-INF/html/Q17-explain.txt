Find all pathways in which a specified kinase participates.<br>
This example finds all pathways the epidermal growth factor receptor (EGFR) participates in. By replacing the line:
<pre>
        filter(STR(?geneName) = 'EGFR')
</pre>
with:
<pre>
        filter(STR(?geneName) = 'BRAF')
</pre>
we instead find all pathways in which the serine/threonine protein kinase B-raf (BRAF) participates.
